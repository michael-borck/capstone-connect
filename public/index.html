About Capstone Connect

Bridging Industry and Education

Capstone Connect is a comprehensive platform designed to connect Curtin University's final-year students with real-world industry challenges. Our system facilitates meaningful partnerships between students and industry clients, creating opportunities for practical learning and innovation.

Platform Features

  • Project Management: Streamlined project submission and management for industry partners
  • Student Matching: Smart matching system connecting students with relevant projects
  • Progress Tracking: Real-time monitoring of project milestones and deliverables
  • Showcase Gallery: Public showcase of completed projects and student achievements
  • Multi-phase Support: Support for complex, multi-semester project structures

Our Mission

To create meaningful connections between academic learning and industry practice, ensuring students graduate with real-world experience while helping businesses solve genuine challenges through fresh perspectives and innovative approaches.

Who We Serve

Students

Gain practical experience working on real industry challenges while building portfolios and professional networks.

Industry Partners

Access fresh talent and innovative solutions while contributing to the development of future professionals.

University

Strengthen industry partnerships while ensuring students receive high-quality, practical education experiences.
